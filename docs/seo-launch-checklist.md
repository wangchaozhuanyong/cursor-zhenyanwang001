# SEO Launch Checklist

1. Check homepage `/` title.
2. Check homepage meta description.
3. Check homepage canonical.
4. Check `og:title`, `og:description`, `og:image`, `og:site_name`.
5. Check WebSite JSON-LD.
6. Check Organization JSON-LD.
7. Check normal product Product JSON-LD.
8. Check restricted product `noindex,follow`.
9. Check restricted product has no Product JSON-LD.
10. Check `/sitemap.xml`.
11. Check `/robots.txt`.
12. Check favicon output.
13. Check apple-touch-icon output.
14. Check `google-site-verification` meta (only when configured).
15. Check `/search` is `noindex,follow`.
16. Check `/cart`, `/checkout`, `/orders`, `/profile`, `/admin` are noindex.
17. Check sitemap excludes restricted products.
18. Check sitemap excludes search/admin/user pages.
19. Check homepage first-screen has real H1 and business text.
20. Check FAQ content replaced with full 大马通 version.
21. Check footer includes links:
    - 隐私政策
    - 用户协议
    - 退款与售后政策
    - 配送说明
    - 合规与年龄限制说明
22. Check siteInfo/admin settings can configure:
    - domain
    - customer service
    - social links
    - OG
    - favicon
    - GSC verification
23. Search codebase: no `flashcast.com.my` (canonical production: `damatong.net`).
24. Search codebase: no `真烟网`.
25. Confirm legal pages are CMS-editable:
    - `/content/privacy-policy`
    - `/content/terms-of-service`
    - `/content/refund-policy`
    - `/content/shipping-policy`
    - `/content/compliance-notice`
